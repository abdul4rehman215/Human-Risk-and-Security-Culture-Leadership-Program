## 1Ô∏è‚É£ What is the Golden Circle model and why is it important in security communication?

The Golden Circle model (Why ‚Üí How ‚Üí What), developed by Simon Sinek, focuses on communicating purpose first.  

In security culture:

- **Why** = Strategic importance (risk reduction, trust, resilience)
- **How** = Implementation methods (training, simulations, communication)
- **What** = Measurable outcomes (metrics, KPIs, ROI)

It is important because executives respond to strategy, managers respond to enablement, and employees respond to clarity and relevance.
