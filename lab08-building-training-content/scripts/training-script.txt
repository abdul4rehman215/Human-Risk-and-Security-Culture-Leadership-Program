PHISHING AWARENESS TRAINING - 5 MINUTES

Introduction (30s):
"Welcome to phishing awareness training. Learn to identify and respond to phishing attacks."

What is Phishing? (60s):
"Phishing: cyber attacks impersonating legitimate organizations to steal credentials and data."

Warning Signs (90s):
1. Urgent or threatening language
2. Generic greetings
3. Suspicious sender addresses
4. Unexpected attachments
5. Requests for sensitive information

Real Examples (120s):
"Examine actual phishing emails and identify red flags."

Conclusion (30s):
"When in doubt, verify independently. Report suspicious emails to IT security."
