# Human Risk Assessment Framework

## Risk Categories
1. **Behavioral Risks**: Password management, email security, social engineering susceptibility
2. **Knowledge Gaps**: Security policy awareness, threat recognition, incident reporting
3. **Environmental Factors**: Security culture, training effectiveness, management support

## Assessment Methodology
- Phase 1: Data Collection (surveys, observations, interviews)
- Phase 2: Risk Analysis (scoring, trend identification)
- Phase 3: Reporting (visualization, recommendations)

## Success Metrics
- Response rate: >80%
- Risk score improvements: >25%
- Incident reduction: >40%
